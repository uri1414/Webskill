# Product

<!-- impeccable:product-schema 1 -->

## Platform

web

## Users

Three audiences, led by residential:
1. **Homeowners & renters** in Albany OR and the surrounding Mid-Willamette Valley who want recurring or one-time house cleaning from a trustworthy, vetted local crew.
2. **Commercial facility & office managers** who need dependable, contracted janitorial service for offices, retail, and medical/professional buildings.
3. **Government / public-sector facility managers** (city, county, state buildings) who require licensed, insured, background-checked contract cleaning that can pass procurement scrutiny.

## Product Purpose

Mid-Valley Facility Services LLC is a cleaning and janitorial company based in Albany, Oregon. The site's job is to earn trust and generate qualified leads (free-quote requests and phone calls) across residential, commercial, and government cleaning work. Success = a visitor understands the three services, believes Mid-Valley is credentialed and local, and requests a quote or calls.

## Positioning

One local, licensed, insured, owner-operated crew that serves homes, businesses, AND government buildings in the Mid-Valley — spanning the trust bar of a maid service and the procurement bar of a facility-services contractor. Not a national franchise; not a bubbly "sparkle" maid brand. Calm, credentialed competence.

## Operating Context

Service area is Albany, Oregon and surrounding Mid-Willamette Valley communities (e.g. Corvallis, Lebanon, Tangent, Millersburg, Jefferson, Salem area). Work is quoted after a walkthrough; recurring and one-time cleaning both offered.

## Capabilities and Constraints

Services: (1) Residential / house cleaning, (2) Commercial / office janitorial, (3) Government / public building cleaning. Static marketing site; the quote form is a front-end lead form (no backend wired yet — submission handler is a placeholder for the user to connect).

## Brand Commitments

- **Name:** Mid-Valley Facility Services LLC.
- **Logo:** provided (`sites/mid-valley/assets/logo.png`) — deep navy wordmark "MID-VALLEY" with sage-green "— FACILITY SERVICES LLC —" subtitle.
- **Brand colors (from logo):** deep navy + muted sage green.
- **Confirmed credentials to feature:** Licensed & Insured, Locally Owned & Operated, Eco-Friendly products/methods.

## Evidence on Hand

- Logo (above).
- Three real photos: residential kitchen cleaning (`assets/residential.webp`), commercial boardroom/office (`assets/commercial.webp`), government/institutional building (`assets/government.webp`).
- **NOT provided — must not be fabricated:** phone number, email, physical address, license/bond numbers, years in business, review counts, testimonials, employee counts. Rendered as clearly-marked placeholders for the owner to replace.

## Product Principles

1. Trust before charm — credentials and locality lead; no hype or cartoon "sparkle" tropes.
2. Serve all three audiences without diluting any; residential leads the narrative.
3. Every conversion path is one step from view: a visible phone number and a free-quote form.
4. Real photography over stock clichés; show the actual work.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Public-sector audience implies a practical WCAG AA bar: sufficient contrast, keyboard-operable form and nav, semantic structure, reduced-motion support.
